## 📜 Overview

StrategyAI is open source under the **AGPL v3 license** — free forever for individuals, researchers, non-profits, and open-source projects.

## ❓ FAQ

### Can I use StrategyAI for my personal trading?
**Yes!** AGPL v3 covers personal use completely. No commercial license needed.

### Can I use StrategyAI in my hedge fund?
**Yes, if** you keep modifications open-source under AGPL v3. If you want to keep improvements proprietary, you need a commercial license.

### Can I host StrategyAI as a SaaS for my clients?
**Only if** you open-source your entire SaaS under AGPL v3. Otherwise, you need a commercial license.

### What if I just want to use the AI code generation, not the backtesting?
Same rules apply. The entire codebase is under AGPL v3.

### Can I resell StrategyAI as-is?
**No.** You need an OEM/White-Label license for resale rights.
