## 🎬 Recording Tips

### Before Recording:
1. **Clean up your desktop** — close unnecessary tabs
2. **Use dark mode** — looks more professional
3. **Test audio** — make sure microphone is clear
4. **Prepare the demo** — have data loaded and ready

### During Recording:
1. **Speak slowly and clearly** — nerves make us talk fast
2. **Pause between sections** — easier to edit
3. **Don't worry about mistakes** — you can re-record that section
4. **Keep mouse movements smooth** — no jerky movements

### After Recording:
1. **Trim dead space** — cut long pauses at start/end
2. **Add background music** (optional) — low volume instrumental
3. **Add captions** (optional) — helps with accessibility
4. **Export as MP4** — 1080p, 30fps
